## ğŸ“ Penjelasan Materi

### Apa itu Infinitive?

**Infinitive** adalah bentuk dasar kata kerja yang didahului oleh kata "**to**".

```
Formula: to + kata kerja dasar
Contoh: to watch, to eat, to go, to study
```

#### ğŸ” Kapan Menggunakan Infinitive?

Infinitive digunakan setelah beberapa kata kerja tertentu, seperti:
- **like** (suka)
- **want** (ingin)
- **need** (perlu)
- **decide** (memutuskan)
- **plan** (merencanakan)

### Bentuk-Bentuk "To Watch"

| Bentuk | Penggunaan | Contoh Kalimat |
|--------|-----------|----------------|
| **To watch** | Infinitive (setelah kata kerja tertentu) | I want **to watch** a movie |
| **Watch** | Bare infinitive / imperative | Please **watch** carefully |
| **Watches** | Present tense (he/she/it) | She **watches** TV every day |
| **Watching** | Gerund / Present continuous | I am **watching** now |
| **Watched** | Past tense / Past participle | I **watched** yesterday |
